Gene Expression View
====================

The Gene Expression View allows you to view pan-genome gene expression
in SAM or silique tissue and view population gene expression in SAM and
silique tissues. At the left section, you can change row and column
order by click different buttons. Users can also hover the heatmap to
view the detail information.
